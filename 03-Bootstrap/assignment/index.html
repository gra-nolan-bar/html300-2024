Welcome to the Dinosaur Page

Dinosaurs are a diverse group of reptiles that dominated the Earth during the Mesozoic Era, living for over 180 million years. They ranged from small, bird-like creatures to massive giants like the Tyrannosaurus rex, a fearsome predator.

The age of dinosaurs began in the Triassic Period and ended in the Cretaceous Period, when a mass extinction event led to their demise. Scientists study fossils to understand how these creatures lived and evolved over millions of years.
